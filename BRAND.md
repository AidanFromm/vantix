# Vantix Brand Guide

- **Primary Color:** Green-teal with black
- **Mode:** Dark only
- **Feel:** Minimal, clean, organized, techie, bold
- **Inspiration:** edwinle.com (smooth, minimal animations)

- Sneaker stores with bad inventory systems
- Businesses with outdated websites
- Anyone needing an all-in-one tech system

1. **Cost** - More affordable than agencies
2. **Speed** - Under 3 weeks (excluding complications)
3. **Reliability** - We deliver what we promise
